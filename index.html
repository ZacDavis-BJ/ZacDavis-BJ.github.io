Mossfinder

Mossfinder is a project dedicated to proving that sustainability and the outdoors lifestyle are accessible to people of all lifepaths and incomes. Together we can explore, create, and cultivate community!

What do we do?

Here at Mossfinder we seek to build an online community based on principles of kindness and creativity! We do this through blogs, podcasts, merchandise, and community events based on our three pillars!
Explore

We accept a challenge to go outside, find new places, and leave no trace! We encourage those who follow along with us to find new places they love to hike, boat, fish, or more. We also encourage the principle of "leave no trace." For all of us this is a principle that displays good stewardship of the world we've been given to explore.

Create

In every person is a creative spark, and we want to help people find theirs! We look to support local artists and community members through our merch store, and by sharing their stories!

Cultivate

Community is something that has been widely impacted by the digital age and world events. As such we seek to use our online and physical presence to build a community of friends who seek peace and joy.

Hiking Recommendations:

Plan ahead:

Think ahead about where you're going! What will the weather be like? How should you dress? How much food should you bring? These are all great questions to ask!

Go as a group:

One of the best recommendations we can give is that you don't go it alone. For one, it's a great way to build community and share time with friends and family! Second, the more people who know where you are (and are with you while you do it) the safer the activity is for everyone!

Explore:

Don't be afraid to try something new! Whether that's a different trail at a favorite location, a state/national park you've never been to before, or just checking out the many nooks and crannies of the outdoors there is so much variety you can introduce! Get out there and make some adventures!
